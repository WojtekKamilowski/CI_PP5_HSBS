Who We Are

HiStory

We are team of history passionate & professionals who had an idea for a dedicated history book store when studying at the Trinity College Dublin, Department of History. Our goal was to create a unique online space where you can find many books & expand your knowledge either if it is for academic research or purely for leisure. Buying books from our website you support local historicians community, contribute to the development of the history academic field and help us to popularize history. If you are interested to volunteer & actively support our mission read more here.
