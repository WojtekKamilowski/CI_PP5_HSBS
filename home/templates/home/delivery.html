Deliveries Policy for HiStory Book Store

Domestic Shipments

All orders are shipped by post, usually it is 5-7 business days to arrive. Orders placed before 16:00 Irish Standard Time (IST) will be shipped out the same day, and orders placed after 16:00 Irish Standard Time (IST)will be shipped out the next business day.

European Union Shipments

All orders are shipped by post, usually it is 5-10 business days to arrive. (depending on the destination country) Orders placed before 16:00 Irish Standard Time (IST) will be shipped out the same day, and orders placed after 16:00 Irish Standard Time (IST)will be shipped out the next business day.

Non-EU Shipments

All orders are shipped by a courier-service, usually it is 5-14 business days to arrive. (depending on thedestination country) Orders placed before 16:00 Irish Standard Time (IST) will be shipped out the same day, and orders placed after 16:00 Irish Standard Time (IST)will be shipped out the next business day. HiStory Book Store is not responsible for any import customs duties/ formalities that may apply in the country of destination. All oders will by shipped using Delivery at Place (DAP) Incoterms 2020 terms. The buyer is responsible for paying any additional charges for import customs clearance.
