HiStory Club

Take an action!

Our HiStory Club is a non-profit organization whose goal is to contribute the history. We are active in various ways, examples of our activities are:

  • History monuments Restorations,
  • Writing petitions to various governmental/non-governmental organizations to fund new history monuments in the forgotten places of important events/ history figures,
  • Organizing crowd-funding actions to support archaeological works,
  • Holding seminars/ webinars on various historical subjects.

If you are interested let us know & join us, using our contact form. We are looking for all kinds of people with different knowledge/skills background, it is enough if you are simply interested in subjects related to history.
